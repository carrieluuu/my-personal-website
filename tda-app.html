Together we're stronger.

The Diabetes App is a free-to-use social media application with over 12K users that is created solely for individuals impacted by diabetes, including type 1, type 2, gestational, and supporters such as family members and friends. The Diabetes App allows users to create a profile and find a community of individuals just like them! The goal is to uplift and empower the diabetes community by providing them with a platform to connect, interact, and learn.
